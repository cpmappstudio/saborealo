Saborealo bakery restaurant overview

Saborealo BakeryRecommendedRestaurant Guru2025

The City of Doral FL has been home to our store for over 17 years. Initially, we were located inside a service station where we offered our customers a menu more oriented to bakery products, hamburgers, hot dogs and breakfasts. It was usual every morning, long lines of loyal customers, eager to carry their cachitos, tequeños and empanadas with coffee or freshly squeezed orange juice.

At night, PANNA Cafe Express, as the place was known, became the meeting point for many residents who returned from their jobs and stopped to buy hamburgers or hot dogs. The high volume of sales and visits allowed in 2020 to move and expand the operation to a much larger and more comfortable space for all Clients with:

  • Casual and modern atmosphere.
  • Commodities furniture.
  • TV screens and high-end sound system
  • The best staff always eager to please and give you the best experience.
